About-Me

I am a Graphic Designer with 4 years of digital and print experience and skills in Web Development. My Web Development skills include HTML, CSS, Responsive Web Design, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, MySQL, Express.js, Mongo, and React.js. I am a creative-collaborator that brings problem-solving skills and outside thinking to the table. I seek to use my knowledge of typography, color theory, visual hierarchy, strengths in time management and meeting deadlines to create the best user-experience possible.
